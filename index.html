Why sign up?

Malware Analysis Learners is a textboard community of malware analysts for all levels of expertise, from “aren't binaries just numbers?” to Pancake or MalwareTech themselves. We have a wide range of boards, including but not limited to:

  • Open Source Resouces (getting started and advanced)
  • General Q&A and discussion (no shitposting)
  • Shitposting general: Anything (legal) goes
  • Tool discussion (Radare2, IDA Pro, etc)
  • The Law: Is this legal?

An account is necessary. But, it is free and always will be. We will never have a monetary barrier anywhere on our website.
